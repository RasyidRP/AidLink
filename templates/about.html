About AidLink

Bridging the gap between survivors and aid providers during critical times

AidLink is an innovative platform developed by University of Toronto first-year students to revolutionize disaster response and aid distribution. Our mission is to create a seamless connection between those in need and those who can help.

For Survivors

  • Real-time resource location
  • Emergency shelter finder
  • Quick need reporting system
  • Live evacuation routes

For Aid Providers

  • Resource management dashboard
  • Distribution optimization
  • Volunteer coordination
  • Real-time demand tracking

Our Mission

To provide an efficient and user-friendly platform that streamlines disaster response, ensuring vital resources reach those in need quickly and effectively.

Our Vision

To revolutionize disaster response by leveraging technology to create stronger, more resilient communities through improved aid distribution and coordination.
